Logo

Currently, for now, temporarily, for the time being, our logo is the BTCRIT Hamster.

The logo is 200px by 200px, though due to its SVG nature, it can be scaled to any size. Below 32px, detail is lost - at small sizes, a square of accent color is preffered. Around the Logo, at least 1em of padding is preffered.

Appropriate backgrounds include Dark, DarkGray, and Base. See the Color Pallet section for color defenitions.
